Motherboard scrapbook competition

In my second semester, my group mates and I embarked on an exciting project called the "Motherboard Scrapbook". The concept behind this project was to create an interactive digital book that would not only educate users about the intricate components of a motherboard but also provide a fun and engaging learning experience. Our goal was to make learning about technology feel like flipping through a playful and informative scrapbook! The Motherboard Scrapbook is a blend of learning and entertainment, designed to teach users about motherboard components, their functions, and the complex world of computer hardware.

Duration: 2023
